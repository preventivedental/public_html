After Your Cleaning

Feeling fresh? Here's what to know after a cleaning — and when to reach out.

Totally Normal

  • Your teeth feel super slick or “different”
  • Your gums are a little sore or tender
  • You notice a bit of bleeding when brushing/flossing

What to Watch

  • Sensitivity that lingers more than a few days
  • Gums that bleed heavily or look swollen
  • Spots that hurt to brush even after a week

Keep It Going

  • Floss daily (yes, really 😉)
  • Use a soft brush — gentle wins
  • Don’t wait too long for your next visit
