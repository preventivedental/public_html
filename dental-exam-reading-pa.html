Worried Something Might Be Off? Let’s Take a Look.

You’re not in a full-blown emergency, but something’s been bugging you. Maybe it’s a dull ache, a weird sensation, a bad taste, or just a gut feeling that something’s not right. You’re not sure if it’s serious, but it’s lingering in the back of your mind and that’s enough of a reason to get it checked out.

This visit is built for moments like that. No drama, no pressure. Just answers. It’s not a full treatment plan. It’s a check-in. A look. A scan. A conversation.

This Is What I Call a Clarity Visit

You’ll come in and we’ll talk about what’s going on. We'll take a look, get X-rays if they’re needed, and explain everything in plain language. CBCT (3D imaging) isn’t part of this visit. We only use it when needed, and only if you agree. It’s billed separately.

You’re still in control. We'll show you what we see, give you a clear path forward, and you decide if or when to move forward. No pressure. No surprises.

What This Visit Isn’t

This isn’t a full workup or cleaning. It’s not about planning every detail or starting treatment right away. It’s not a commitment to move forward with anything.

This visit is just about understanding what’s going on.

We’ll take a look, talk it through, and help you make sense of it without pressure.

If it turns out something needs more attention, we’ll explain what’s next and let you decide the pace.

You Don’t Have to Know What It Is

Most people who come in for this kind of visit say things like, “It’s not that bad, I just want to be safe,” or “I don’t want this to turn into a bigger problem.” Some haven’t been to the dentist in years. Some come in every six months. This isn’t about being perfect. It’s about being smart with your health.

Whether it’s something or nothing, at least you’ll know. And for a lot of people, that peace of mind is worth way more than the price of the visit.

Why We Offer This

Too many folks wait until they’re in serious pain because they’re afraid of the cost, the judgment, or the unknown. This visit removes all of that. It’s simple, it’s honest, and it gets you answers without locking you into anything.

Even if you decide to wait, or do nothing, you’ll leave with clarity and that alone makes a difference.

Let’s Make It Easy

The visit is $150 flat. That includes the exam, any necessary 2D X-rays, and a straight-up conversation about what’s going on. If we need a CBCT scan, that’s a separate thing. We’ll explain why, and you’ll know the cost before anything happens.
