Before Your Cleaning

What to expect, how to get the most from your visit, and a few things to prep.

Bring These With You

  • Any updated prescriptions or medical info
  • A list of any concerns, sensitive spots, or goals

We May Need X-Rays

  • They help us catch problems early — even the ones we can't see just by looking.
  • If it’s been a while, we’ll likely recommend taking a few.

Insurance Info? Let’s Get Ahead of It

  • If your insurance changed, bring your new card with you
  • Better yet — text or email it to us before your visit
  • We like clean teeth, not surprise bills 😅

Let’s Talk About It

  • We love it when you bring questions
  • If you have a concern, let us know — we might be able to handle it today
Can’t make it? Text or call us — we’ll help you find a time that works.

We’ll keep things simple and clear — no lectures, no pressure. Just real care that makes sense.
