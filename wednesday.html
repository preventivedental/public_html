What we do vs. what we do not do

This page is for pain-to-resolution patients. It is not for complex restorative planning.

We are your best bet if...

  • You are in pain and want the tooth gone today.
  • The tooth is visible or broken at the gumline.
  • You want a fast, no-frills procedure.
  • You can tolerate a normal numbing shot.

We are not for you if...

  • You want to save the tooth with a root canal or crown.
  • The tooth is fully buried under bone or an impacted wisdom tooth.
  • No bone grafting: if you are planning a future dental implant, you should see a restorative specialist for the extraction so the bone is preserved for their needs.
  • You need sedation or "going under" to get through treatment.

How this block works

Simple, fast, and direct. This is not a long consult.

1. Walk in Wednesday morning Arrive during the 8am-12pm block. It is first come, first served.
2. We confirm the case fits We examine the tooth, take x-rays, and make sure this is an extraction case we can safely handle. If the case is too complex for our clinic, we will provide a referral to a specialist. Exam and x-ray fees still apply.
3. If it fits, we remove the tooth The goal is relief. We numb the area, remove the tooth, review aftercare, and get you on with your day.
