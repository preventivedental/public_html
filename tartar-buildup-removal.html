When DIY Is Okay — and When It’s Time to Come In

You can do a lot at home to keep your teeth clean — brushing, flossing, and yes, even using a gentle at-home scraper for light buildup or stain. If it flakes off easily and you’re not pressing hard, that’s fine.

But if you’re scrubbing and nothing’s budging — or you feel like you’re working too hard — it’s time to let us handle it. Tartar that’s hardened won’t come off safely at home, and trying too hard can damage your enamel or gums.

When in doubt, let us check. We’ll tell you what’s safe to do yourself and what’s better left to us. No judgment — just real help.

What a Cleaning With Us Is Really Like

No lectures. No shame. No surprises. We start by seeing what’s going on — buildup, inflammation, or trouble spots. Most people leave saying, “That wasn’t bad at all.” You’ll walk out feeling fresher — and knowing what’s really going on with your teeth.
