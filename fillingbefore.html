After Your Tooth Extraction

What to expect, how to heal well, and when to call us.

What to Expect:

  • The visit usually takes about 30–60 minutes
  • We’ll numb the area so it’s comfortable
  • You can drive yourself home unless we tell you otherwise

Helpful Reminders:

  • Eat a little something beforehand — you’ll be numb after
  • Brush + floss before your visit like normal
  • Let us know if you’re nervous — we’ve got options to help

Insurance Info?

If you're not sure we have your current insurance info, please send it! It helps avoid surprises.
