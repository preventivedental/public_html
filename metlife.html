METLIFE PATIENT NOTICE

We're no longer in-network with MetLife as of 12/14/2025.

Oct 06, 2025
My office will end in-network participation with MetLife effective 12/14/2025. From this point forward, I'll be considered out-of-network. You can still be seen here, and I’m happy to continue your care. MetLife will now reimburse you directly.

Here’s why this change is happening:

MetLife hasn’t kept up with the times. Below are actual fees they’ve paid me, comparing 2011 to 2025:

Inflation and cost increases since 2011: over 40%.

If these fees had simply kept pace with real-world costs, they’d be 40–60% higher today. Instead, MetLife’s reimbursements remain nearly flat.

In real terms, it’s less money today than it was over a decade ago.

Meanwhile, MetLife has:

  • Downcoded procedures to pay less
  • Bundled x-rays to avoid fair reimbursement
  • Claimed their fees are competitive while cutting them every year

I’ve absorbed their discount for as long as I could. But continuing at these rates just isn’t sustainable anymore.

If you have MetLife, you’re still welcome here. Nothing about the way I treat you changes. But the billing relationship does: MetLife will pay you, and you’ll pay my office directly.

I know this won’t sit well with everyone. I just want to be straight with you about why this change is happening and how it affects your care.
