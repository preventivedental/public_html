FIDELIO PATIENT NOTICE

We're ending in-network participation with Fidelio Dental Insurance.

October 6, 2025
My office will no longer be in-network with Fidelio Dental. I’m making this transition to ensure I can continue offering care at the level I believe you deserve. You can still be seen here, and I’m happy to continue caring for you. However, Fidelio may now reimburse you directly instead of paying the office.

Why the change?

Fidelio's reimbursement schedule hasn’t kept pace with real-world costs. Here are actual examples comparing their allowed fees from 2012 to 2025:

Procedure Code Fidelio 2012 Fidelio 2025 Change
Routine Check-Up D0120 $29 $30 +$1
Adult Cleaning D1110 $61 $62 +$1

Average increase: $1 over 13 years.
Meanwhile, inflation and cost of care have risen over 40% in that same period.

I’ve held off making this change for as long as I reasonably could. The decision was not made lightly, and I know it may be inconvenient for some patients. But it’s the right step to ensure I can continue focusing on care and not absorbing unsustainable costs.

Here’s what this means for you:

  • You are still welcome in this office. Nothing about your care will change.
  • Fidelio may now reimburse you directly instead of paying the office.
  • You’ll pay my office at the time of service unless we arrange otherwise.

Total cost for a typical six-month check-up and cleaning (exam D0120 + adult cleaning D1110) is now $195.
