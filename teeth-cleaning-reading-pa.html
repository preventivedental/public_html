Why You’re Noticing It Now

If your teeth feel rough, your breath is off, or you looked in the mirror and thought, “Alright, something’s not right,” you're not the only one. Whether it's been a few months or a few years, this is the moment a lot of people decide to do something about it. We're here to help make that easy.

What That Feeling Tells You

Sometimes your tongue catches a rough spot. Maybe you see buildup between your teeth or notice your breath isn't as fresh. Or maybe you're just tired of putting it off. Whatever the reason, you're not being picky. You're being aware. That’s a good thing.

What to Expect at Your Visit

We’ll start by taking a look and seeing where things stand. If a cleaning can be done that day, we’ll get it done. If there’s inflammation, infection, or something that needs to be handled first, we’ll show you what we see and explain what makes sense. No pressure. No surprises. Just straight answers.

No Shame, No Lectures

A lot of people go longer than they meant to. Life gets busy. Stuff comes up. What matters is that you’re showing up now, and we’ll meet you where you are. No lectures. Just help.

Why People Trust Preventive Dental

We keep it simple. No gimmicks. No pushing things you don’t need. Just honest care, explained clearly. If a same-day cleaning makes sense, we’ll do it. If something needs to be treated first, we’ll walk you through the options. You’re in control either way.

What It Costs

For uninsured or out-of-network patients, most cleanings and exams range from $180 to $250, depending on your needs.

If you’re due for X-rays, they’re included. If there’s more going on (like gum issues or buildup that needs extra time), we’ll let you know beforehand — no surprise charges.

If we’re in-network with your insurance, we’ll handle it directly and review coverage and estimated costs with you first.
