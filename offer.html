What’s bothering you

The stuff you notice. The stuff that keeps you up. We help make sense of it.

Pain that won’t quit

Sharp, throbbing, pressure, or “just wrong.” We find the cause and help calm it down.

Cracked or broken teeth

Something chipped, split, or feels fragile when you bite. We stabilize it first, then talk next steps.

Swelling or pressure

Jaw, face, or gum swelling is your body asking for help. We rule out infection and act quickly.

Bleeding gums

Blood when brushing or flossing is not normal. We explain why it’s happening and how to stop it.

Bad breath that keeps coming back

If it’s not food, something else is going on. We track down the source and deal with it.

Just knowing something’s off

No pain yet, but something doesn’t feel right. We take a look, explain what we see, and let you decide.

If any of this sounds familiar, you’re in the right place.

Questions, Appointments, and Payment

Frequently Asked Questions

When can I get an appointment?
We try to get you in fast often the same day. If it's after hours, we'll follow up first thing in the morning, next working day.

If you leave a message, send an email, or text, we'll get back to you as soon as we can. Sometimes we're with patients, but we do check everything regularly.

If it's urgent and can't wait, use the emergency option on our phone line. That gets flagged for quicker attention.

What if I'm nervous about dental work?
Dental work doesn't have to be stressful. We'll walk you through every step and make sure you're good before we do anything.

What if I need a root canal or crown?
If you need more advanced care, we'll explain why, lay out the options, and let you decide. No stress. No pressure. Just facts.

How do I know if my insurance covers this?
We'll confirm your coverage before doing anything and handle the paperwork for you. No surprises.

What if I don't have insurance?
Doesn't matter. We'll go over your cost and any payment options upfront. Nothing starts until you're on board. No commitments without your go-ahead.

Will you tell me my cost upfront?
Always. No hidden fees. No guesswork. You'll know before we start.
