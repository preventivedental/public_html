After Your Filling

What’s normal, what to do at home,
and when to reach out.

What’s Totally Normal:

  • Feeling numb for a few hours
  • Mild soreness near the injection site
  • A little pressure when biting — your bite may need fine-tuning

Things to Watch For:

  • Bite feels off or “high” after numbness wears off
  • Lingering sensitivity to hot/cold after a week
  • Pain when chewing or lasting discomfort

Pro Tips:

  • Wait until numbness wears off before chewing
  • Keep brushing and flossing like normal
  • If we numbed your lower jaw, watch your cheek/lip — don’t chew it
